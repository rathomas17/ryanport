About

Hi, my name is Ryan-Alexander Thomas. I love teaching, coding, and music!

Student

I am a Computer Science and Engineering student at The Ohio State University having transferred from University of Houston! Before programming, I studied Music Theoory, Composition, and Audio Engineering; I earned my Teacher's Certification in 2013.

Developer

I've been programming for almost two years now. Even though I was mostly self-taught, I was able to garner my first programming job at MIT. Since then, my college-level coursework includes: C++, Java, and Software I.

Musician

I've been playing instruments since I was young; the tenor saxophone is my go-to. However, I spent years playing piano while teaching and directing the band at St. Theresa. My favorite instrument to play is the ukulele, and my favorite to listen to is the bassoon.

Entrepreneur

By Fall 2016, I had raised over $250,000, hired 12 employees, and looking into procuring a second office for our growing software engineering company.
